Collaboration in Legal Bundles

How BundleCorp Facilitates Teamwork



Lawyers, both transactional and contentious, are almost always required to work collaboratively. The in-house GC, external counsel, barristers and opposing parties often need to review, comment or amend the same documents; a task made more challenging by the increase in remote working. In the digital age, online bundle generating platforms are transforming the way legal professionals work together, making collaboration on documents bundles more accessible, more intuitive, and more efficient.

Let us explore how BundleCorp empowers legal teams to work in harmony.

In-app Collaboration:

We provide a digital platform for legal teams to work together in real-time. Multiple team members can access and work on the same legal bundle simultaneously. This function eliminates the need for time-consuming meetings, the possibility of error in version control, and allows everyone to stay on the same page even when working from different locations.

Annotation and Commenting:

  • Collaboration often involves discussions and feedback on specific documents. With us, users can annotate documents, add comments or queries, and highlight crucial information. This feature streamlines communication and ensures that everyone's insights are captured directly within the document bundle.

Centralised Communication:

    Traditional collaboration methods, such as email chains, can lead to scattered and fragmented communication. We centralise communication within the platform. Team members can discuss documents, make decisions, and address concerns without the need for external communication tools.

Secure Sharing:

Sharing document bundles securely with external parties is critical, as is maintaining privilege over communications with the client. We facilitate this through a secure sharing feature, which means that annotation and comments on bundles are shared with specified recipients only. Lawyers can confidently share required information with clients, experts and barristers, whilst maintaining control over access.

BundleCorp's collaborative features empower legal teams to work seamlessly and efficiently. Whether you are a small team or a large department, our platform streamlines teamwork, fosters clear communication, and provides tools that simplify the complexity of collaboration.

With BundleCorp, we want to ensure that the lawyers focus less on managing the bundle, and more on leveraging the bundle as a tool to build robust strategies and deliver exceptional results.
